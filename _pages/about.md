I am Anoop T. V., an Associate Professor in the [Department of Mathematics at  Indian Institute of Technology Madras](https://math.iitm.ac.in/innerfaculty.php?fname=Anoop%20T%20V). My broad research interests include
isoperimetric inequalities for the eigenvalues and weighted Hardy-Sobolev inequalities.
